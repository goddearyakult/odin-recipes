3 Ingredient Chocolate Cherry Dump Cake

A Chocolatey Cherry Cake.

This 3-ingredient chocolate cherry dump cake could not be easier! Just pie filling, chocolate cake mix, and butterâ€”you'll have it ready to bake before you can say, 'preheated oven'.

Ingredients.

  • 2 (595 g each) cans cherry pie filling
  • 1 (432 g) box chocolate cake mix
  • 113 g butter

Steps.

  1. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees C. Pour pie filling into a 23 x 33 baking dish (or similar sized rectangular dish).
  2. Spread cake mix evenly over the pie filling, take care not to mix it into the fruit. Top evenly with butter slices.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly golden brown and bubbly, about 45 minutes. Let stand at least 5 minutes before serving.
